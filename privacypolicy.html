Privacy Policy | The Thread Co.

Last Updated: July 2026

The Thread Co. respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This policy outlines how we handle your data when you visit or shop on our platform.



1. What We Collect

We collect basic personal and technical details to facilitate your shopping experience, including:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, physical shipping address, phone number, and order details provided during checkout or when contacting customer service.
  • Automated & Technical Data: Cookies and tracking technologies that collect your browser type, device information, and IP address to help improve website navigation and store performance.

Note on Payment Security: We handle your data securely. Your credit card and payment details are processed exclusively through encrypted, third-party payment gateways (e.g., standard PCI-DSS compliant platforms). We never store your card information.


2. How We Use It

Your information is strictly utilized to deliver a seamless shopping experience:

  • No Selling: We do not sell, rent, or lease your personal data to third parties
  • Service Providers: We only share essential data with trusted partners (such as courier/shipping companies and payment gateways) strictly to fulfill your order.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required by law or to protect the safety and rights of our brand and its users.


4. Your Rights & Consent

  • Managing Cookies: You have the option to manage or disable cookie tracking directly through your internet browser settings.
  • Marketing Opt-Out: You can withdraw your consent for email marketing at any time by utilizing the "unsubscribe" link included at the bottom of our promotional emails.
